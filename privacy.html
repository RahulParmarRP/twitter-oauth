Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This app respects your privacy and does not collect or store personal information beyond what is required for authentication.

1. OAuth Tokens

Tokens used for logging in with Twitter are stored only on your device. They are not shared or transmitted to third parties.

2. Twitter Data

Any tweets, user information, or other content retrieved from Twitter is handled according to Twitter’s Privacy Policy.

3. No Tracking

This app does not track your usage or store any analytics data.
